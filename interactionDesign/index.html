What is Interaction ?

Research Task


Interaction can be thought of as the dynamic process in which multiple elements, such as people, groups, objects, systems, etc., interact with each other and actively participate in a mutual exchange of information, energy, influence, emotion or impact. While interaction typically implies a reciprocal exchange between entities, there are situations where the interaction can be one-way or asymmetric. For example, reading a book or viewing the exhibition/artwork involves one-way or asymmetric interactions in which the person receives information or impact without actively participating or engaging with the source.
